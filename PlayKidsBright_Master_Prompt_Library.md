## SECTION 1: PROMPT ENGINEERING FUNDAMENTALS

### 3 Core Rules of Prompt Engineering

1. **Be SPECIFIC** (not vague)
   - ❌ Bad: "Make a video"
   - ✅ Good: "Make 8-second S1 mystery video showing squirrel confused, looking at muddy animal, forest background, no camera zoom"

2. **Give EXAMPLES** (show what you want)
   - ❌ Bad: "Write a caption"
   - ✅ Good: "Write caption like: 'Quack quack! 🦆 Watch squirrel discover...'"

3. **Add CONSTRAINTS** (limits/rules)
   - ❌ Bad: "Make a video"
   - ✅ Good: "Make 8-sec video, 9:16 vertical, Pixar style, forest background, squirrel walks left-to-right, no zoom"

## SECTION 2: VIDEO PROMPT TEMPLATE

### Master Video Prompt Formula

```
[SCENE SETUP]
Location: [forest/ocean/city]
Lighting: [golden sunset/bright morning]
Style: 3D Pixar, 9:16 vertical, no camera zoom

[CHARACTER POSITION & EMOTION]
Squirrel on [LEFT/CENTER/RIGHT], [emotion: confused/happy/shocked]
[Other characters]: [position], [action]

[OBJECTS & BACKGROUND]
Background: [trees/flowers/water]
Foreground: [mud pond/forest floor]
Animation elements: [rain/sparkles/letters]

[CAMERA RULES]
No zoom, no movement, static position
[OR if movement: quick pop-in → smooth slow zoom]

[TEXT OVERLAY]
Letter "X" (GREEN/BLUE, pop-up animation)
Text "Word" (ORANGE/YELLOW, bouncing animation)

[FINAL SPECS]
3D Pixar style, 9:16 vertical, NO text (if audio only)
```

## SECTION 3: AUDIO PROMPT TEMPLATE

### Master Audio Prompt Formula

```
[SOUND EFFECT - 2 seconds]
[Pause - 1-2 seconds]

Squirrel (soft, [emotion]): "[Line 1]"
[Pause - 2 seconds]

Squirrel (soft, [emotion]): "[Line 2]"
[Pause - 2 seconds]

Squirrel (soft, [emotion]): "[Line 3]"

[BACKGROUND SOUND continues OR stops]
```

### Sound Effect Library

- Rustling leaves (A-Z mystery)
- Mud sounds (Zebra S1)
- Rain (Zebra S2)
- Alert/chime (hooks)
- Celebration music (endings)
- Silence (pauses matter!)

### Emotion Keywords for Squirrel

- soft (default for all dialogue)
- confused (mystery scenes)
- excited (discovery)
- happy (joy/celebration)
- shocked (surprise)
- celebrating (final reveal)
- cheerful (encouragement)
- curious (questions)

## SECTION 4: A-Z VIDEO STRUCTURE (PROVEN TEMPLATE)

### S1 - MYSTERY (8 seconds)

**Video Prompt Pattern:**
```
Squirrel STANDING/LOOKING (left side), [confused emotion]
[Animal/object] FAR in distance (right side, blurred)
Squirrel observes, doesn't know what it is
Forest background, colorful flowers, warm sunlight
No camera movement, static position
```

**Audio Pattern:**
```
[Sound effect - 2 sec]
[Pause - 1 sec]
Squirrel (soft, confused): "What is that [thing]?"
[Pause - 2 sec]
Squirrel (soft, curious): "[Guess/question]?"
[Pause - 2 sec]
Squirrel (soft, wondering): "What could it be?"
```

---

### S2 - DISCOVER (8 seconds)

**Video Prompt Pattern:**
```
Squirrel WALKING left-to-right (shocked/amazed expression)
[Animal/object] getting CLOSER (medium distance)
[Action happening: rain/movement/reveal]
Key details appearing (stripes/wings/features)
Same forest background
No camera zoom, static position
```

**Audio Pattern:**
```
[Sound effect continues - 2 sec]
[Pause - 1 sec]
Squirrel (soft, surprised): "Oh! Look [at action]!"
[Pause - 2 sec]
Squirrel (soft, excited): "[Detail being revealed]!"
[Pause - 2 sec]
Squirrel (soft, realization): "[Key observation]!"
```

---

### S3 - ACTION + LEARNING (8 seconds)

**Video Prompt Pattern:**
```
Squirrel POINTING at [animal/object] (happy/celebrating)
[Animal/object] CENTER/CLOSE (fully visible, clean)
Letter "[X]" (GREEN, pop-up bouncing animation)
Text "[Word]" (ORANGE/YELLOW, pop-up bouncing animation)
Forest background, magical lighting
No camera zoom, static position
```

**Audio Pattern:**
```
[Sound effect ends/changes - 1 sec]
[Pause - 1 sec]
Squirrel (soft, happy): "[Final reveal]!"
[Pause - 2 sec]
Squirrel (soft, happy): "[Letter] for [Word]!"
[Pause - 2 sec]
Squirrel (soft, celebrating): "What a [adjective] [animal]!"
```

## SECTION 11: BRAND GUIDELINES (REFERENCE)

### Visual Rules

- ✅ Squirrel: Blue, Pixar-style, walks LEFT-to-RIGHT
- ✅ Birds: Fly RIGHT-to-LEFT (opposite direction = dynamic)
- ✅ Forest: Colorful flowers, red-white mushrooms, green trees
- ✅ Lighting: Warm golden sunset (consistent)
- ✅ Format: 9:16 vertical (all videos)
- ✅ Camera: No zoom, no movement (static)
- ✅ Scene duration: 8 seconds per scene

### Text Animation

- ✅ S3 text: Bouncing/pop-up animation
- ✅ Letter color: GREEN (main), BLUE (thumbnails)
- ✅ Word color: ORANGE/YELLOW
- ✅ No text in S1/S2 (audio only)

### Audio Rules

- ✅ Squirrel ONLY talks (no other characters speak)
- ✅ Sound effects SEPARATE from dialogue
- ✅ 2-second pauses between lines
- ✅ Soft tone (designed for toddlers)
- ✅ Simple grammar (kids understand)

## SECTION 14: COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

- ❌ Camera zoom (makes kids dizzy)
- ❌ Text in S1/S2 (breaks mystery)
- ❌ Animals speaking (only squirrel talks)
- ❌ Long pauses (kills engagement)
- ❌ Vague prompts (confusing AI)
- ❌ Inconsistent style (breaks brand)
- ❌ Posting at wrong times (low reach)
- ❌ No CTA (no subscriber growth)
- ❌ Carousel/photos only (use Reels!)
- ❌ Complex English (kids don't understand)
